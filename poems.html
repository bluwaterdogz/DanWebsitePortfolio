Eye of the Storm



Here we sit in the eye of the storm
Sit closer to me and I can keep you warm
Don’t stray too far or I might not see you again
Please stay grounded so we don’t get swept up in the wind
If you get swept up I will lend you my hand
If you reach back to me you will gently land
When the wall of the storm creeps up to our sides
Get ahold of me and we can both catch our eyes
Lightning might strike but it shouldn't have to strike twice
Far off in the distance it might even look nice
The voice of the thunder takes time to hear
The time it takes tells us if lightning is near
Thunder itself is nothing to fear
But keep your eyes open along with your ear
A crack in the sky might cause a huge scene
Hold each other close and the crack won't come between
If rain should fall even through the center of our eye
I will see you through it until it becomes dry.


Mr.Kitty(Dedicated to Toby)



Poor Mr.Kitty He needs his medicine
Left his home loaded on catnip and amphetamines
Chased some rats to the levy downtown
Cat catcher caught up and through him in the pound
Skinny Mr.Kitty with a good head on his shoulders
Gathered a group of fat cats all tough as boulders
The crew of cats creeped through the yard
hustling ketamine to the old saint bernards
Space ran scarce in the animal prison
Soon it came time for lethal injection
The time to escape was now or never
Mr.Kitty assembled his crew for this risky endeavour
Breaching the gates with the reapers at bay
Would take place when night turned today
Mr.Kitty arched his back and crouched to the ground
The crew of cats escaped without making a sound
Back on the streets the kitties got wise
Figured making the best with the rest of their nine lives

Ill lose her never
Cause I always hear her laugh

No Eyes



Have you met the man with no eyes
he searches in circles for directions and guides
he asks for help from the people passing by
they cant really help and they rarely reply
but when they respond they reply with a lie
Have you met the woman who needs a ride
she went down south and always cried
she keeps going even when the south has dried
I couldnt keep up but I still tried
she stole my car and left me on the side
Have you met the boy who never existed
his world is weird not sick just twisted
he needs nothing in life but likes having kisses
he was never born but to the world he was gifted
he loves all thats alive and nothing thats dead
he imagined a girl all in his head
he created my world as he slept in her bed
